1.Take boiled eggs in a bowl.
2.Add ginger-garlic paste, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, salt, cumin powder and coriander powder.
3.Mix the spices thoroughly.
4.Take a pan and add cumin seeds, green chilli and chopped onion
5.Saute them tilll golden brown and then add ginger, garlic, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chilli powder and salt.
6.Now add tomatoes, mix well and add more water. Mix them thoroughly.
7.Grind the mixture to get the perfect mix of spicy gravy.
